Broccoli Cheese Soup

A Delicious Soup for the Hearth

Made from a base of cheddar cheese and spices with a healthy amount of broccoli added for crunch, this broccoli cheese soup is bound to hit the mark every time. These ingredients are a must to create your own.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 onion, chopped (not diced)
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans of chicken broth
  • 1 (1 lb) bag of cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • 2/3 cup of cornstarch
  • 1 c of water

Steps

  1. Gather all of the ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onions and cook, stirring occasionally until the butter is softened. Then stir in broccoli.
  3. Add broth and simmer up to 15 minutes until the broccoli is tender
  4. Reduce heat, add cheese and stir until its melted. Add in milk and garlic powder and keep stirring.
  5. Combine the cornstarch and water in a separate bowl. Once that's done add it to the soup and stir until thickened. Serve and enjoy with bread of choice.
